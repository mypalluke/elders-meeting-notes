Agenda Item 4

Reach the Heart

Topic: Enthusiasm

The Goal: To use animated delivery to show strong feelings about the value of your message.

The Importance: Enthusiasm is highly contagious! It captures attention, brings life to the talk, and motivates listeners to take action.

1 Speak With Feeling

  • Heartfelt Motivation: True speaking stems from an appreciative heart (worship in "spirit and truth"). It naturally shows in your face, gestures, and voice.
  • Live Your Subject: Don't just prep notes; get emotionally invested. Example: When speaking of the ransom, focus on your *personal* gratitude.
  • Prepare Your Heart: Like Ezra, prepare your heart first so listeners develop genuine love for the truth.

2 Think About Your Audience

  • Have Conviction: Believe deeply that the audience *needs* this message. Pray for guidance.
  • Find a Fresh Angle: Material doesn't have to be new, but your approach should be. Focus on how it directly helps them cope or improve their faith.
  • Field Ministry Tip: Equip yourself with Bible solutions to common, current news/problems. It naturally sparks eagerness to share!

3 Animated Delivery

  • Facial Expressions: Animation should be visible on your face. Sound convinced, but not dogmatic.
  • Maintain Balance: Do not overdo it. Being bombastic or overly emotional makes the audience think about *you* instead of the *message*.
  • Overcome Shyness: Like Apollos, combine good audience contact with genuine enthusiasm to move listeners to action.

4 Appropriate to Material

  • Pacing & Variety: High enthusiasm constantly will exhaust the audience. Variety is essential.
  • Save for Climaxes: Save your highest enthusiasm for main points and peaks, especially when motivating application.
  • Keep it Genuine: Never force it; the material dictates the emotion.

Summary: How to Develop It

Involve Emotions: Prepare not only the info, but also your heart.
Focus on Audience: Consider how points directly benefit listeners.
Map It Out: Identify the specific portions that require special enthusiasm.
Be Alive: Speak with strength, ensuring your face reflects the feelings.
